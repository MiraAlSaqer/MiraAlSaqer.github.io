Hi! My name is Mira. I am a game development student at HTU with a deep rooted passion for bringing interactive worlds to life. My journey with video games started when I was very young, playing through different worlds and slowly developing an inkling passion to create them myself, to allow others to experience the same feelings I did through my work. Whether it is a full prototype or a quick mechanical project, I love the process of building systems from the ground up.

I have always loved exploring a vast variety of game genres, which drives me to constantly experiment with distinct features and design elements. I naturally gravitate toward charming themes involving psychology, animals, and food, and I find immense fulfillment when my projects carry a meaningful personal touch derived from the things I love.

These days, my main focus is entirely on sharpening my programming abilities, pushing my prototyping skills forward, and mastering new workflows. I am a firm believer that with the right assets, deep focus, and an ample amount of time, I can confidently turn any creative concept into a polished, functional reality.
